Jareesh

Jareesh is a popular Saudi dish, famous for in the Najd region. The method of cooking it begins by preparing it from crushed durum wheat, popularly known as luqami. Add the crushed or crushed luqami wheat, not ground, to the meat stock and cumin and leave it on low heat for a long time until it becomes like harissa. Add milk to it and serve it hot. Some prefer it. Add butter on top or wild ghee.
